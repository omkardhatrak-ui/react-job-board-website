About JobFinder

A simpler search-to-application experience

JobFinder brings together keyword and location search, nearby filtered listings, application flow, interview prep, and live practice pages in one connected experience. This about page makes the product story visible instead of hiding it behind an empty nav item.

Core focus Job discovery
Primary audience Candidates
Workflow Search to prep

What the project covers

  • Search by role and location with geolocation support
  • Filterable nearby listings with dummy coverage for all combinations
  • Application forms and personal tracking pages
  • Interview prep plus live testing extensions

Why this matters

Small portfolio projects feel dramatically stronger when navigation leads somewhere useful. Replacing dead nav links with real content makes the product easier to understand and the repo easier to present.

Design direction

The site uses a light, glassy visual system with supportive panels, gradients, and practical task pages. This section keeps that same language while giving the nav genuine structure.
